HOW TO USE
----------
These diagrams are wired in js/app.js (ART_ASSETS). They appear on:
  - Homepage category cards
  - Product cards when a piece has no photo
  - Admin "Artwork style" dropdown

When adding a piece in admin.html, pick the matching artwork style.
The diagram shows automatically when the piece has no uploaded photo.
